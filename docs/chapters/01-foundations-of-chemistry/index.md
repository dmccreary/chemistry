# Chapter 1: Foundations of Chemistry

## Summary

This chapter establishes the foundational scientific skills needed for AP Chemistry, including measurement, dimensional analysis, significant figures, and data analysis. Students will build the quantitative reasoning and laboratory skills that underpin all subsequent chemistry content.
