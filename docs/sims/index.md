# List of MicroSims for AP Chemistry

Interactive Micro Simulations to help students learn AP Chemistry fundamentals.

<div class="grid cards" markdown>

-   **[Arrhenius Equation Explorer](./arrhenius-equation-explorer/index.md)**

    ![Arrhenius Equation Explorer](./arrhenius-equation-explorer/arrhenius-equation-explorer.png)

    Adjust activation energy, frequency factor, and temperature range to compare rate constants and Arrhenius plots in real time.

-   **[Atomic Structure Diagram](./atomic-structure-diagram/index.md)**

    ![Atomic Structure Diagram](./atomic-structure-diagram/atomic-structure-diagram.png)

    Interactive MicroSim showing protons, neutrons, and electron shells for elements Z=1–18. Adjust the atomic number slider to explore how subatomic particles define each element.

-   **[Beer-Lambert Law Calibration Curve Builder](./beer-lambert-calibration/index.md)**

    ![Beer-Lambert Law Calibration Curve Builder](./beer-lambert-calibration/beer-lambert-calibration.png)

    Build a spectrophotometry calibration curve, compute slope and R², and solve for an unknown concentration using Beer-Lambert relationships.

-   **[Buffer pH Interactive Calculator](./buffer-ph-calculator/index.md)**

    ![Buffer pH Interactive Calculator](./buffer-ph-calculator/buffer-ph-calculator.png)

    Use Henderson-Hasselbalch to compute buffer pH or find the [A⁻]/[HA] ratio needed for a target pH, with contextual visuals.

-   **[Buffer System Visualizer](./buffer-visualizer/index.md)**

    ![Buffer System Visualizer](./buffer-visualizer/buffer-visualizer.png)

    Explore how HA/A⁻ ratios and strong acid/base additions influence buffer and plain water pH using Henderson-Hasselbalch.

-   **[Catalyzed vs. Uncatalyzed Energy Profile](./catalysis-energy-profile/index.md)**

    ![Catalyzed vs. Uncatalyzed Energy Profile](./catalysis-energy-profile/catalysis-energy-profile.png)

    Compare activation energies for catalyzed and uncatalyzed pathways with adjustable catalyst strength, enthalpy, and intermediate visualization.

-   **[Dimensional Analysis Practice](./dimensional-analysis-practice/index.md)**

    ![Dimensional Analysis Practice](./dimensional-analysis-practice/dimensional-analysis-practice.png)

    Interactive conversion builder for unit analysis with multi-level problems, scoring, and auto unit cancellation hints.

-   **[Dissolution Equilibrium Visualizer](./dissolution-equilibrium-visualizer/index.md)**

    ![Dissolution Equilibrium Visualizer](./dissolution-equilibrium-visualizer/dissolution-equilibrium-visualizer.png)

    Watch a sparingly soluble salt reach dynamic equilibrium, track Q vs Ksp, and test the common-ion effect with animated ions.

-   **[Electrolysis Stoichiometry Calculator](./faraday-law-calculator/index.md)**

    ![Electrolysis Stoichiometry Calculator](./faraday-law-calculator/faraday-law-calculator.png)

    Use Faraday's law to solve for deposited mass, time, or current in electrolytic systems with step-by-step visuals.

-   **[Galvanic Cell Visualizer](./galvanic-cell-visualizer/index.md)**

    ![Galvanic Cell Visualizer](./galvanic-cell-visualizer/galvanic-cell-visualizer.png)

    Animate zinc-copper style galvanic cells with salt bridge flow, electron movement, and live cell notation plus Nernst-based potentials.

-   **[Gibbs Free Energy Spontaneity Explorer](./gibbs-free-energy-explorer/index.md)**

    ![Gibbs Free Energy Spontaneity Explorer](./gibbs-free-energy-explorer/gibbs-free-energy-explorer.png)

    Manipulate enthalpy, entropy, and temperature to visualize ΔG vs. temperature, sign quadrants, and crossover points for spontaneity.

-   **[Hess's Law Pathway Visualizer](./hess-law-visualizer/index.md)**

    ![Hess's Law Pathway Visualizer](./hess-law-visualizer/hess-law-visualizer.png)

    Compare direct and multi-step reaction pathways to see that total enthalpy change is path independent.

-   **[ICE Table Interactive Solver](./ice-table-solver/index.md)**

    ![ICE Table Interactive Solver](./ice-table-solver/ice-table-solver.png)

    Step through equilibrium problems with dynamic ICE tables, approximation checks, and quadratic solving guidance.

-   **[Ideal Gas Law Interactive Simulator](./ideal-gas-law-simulator/index.md)**

    ![Ideal Gas Law Interactive Simulator](./ideal-gas-law-simulator/ideal-gas-law-simulator.png)

    Adjust P, V, T, and n to explore PV = nRT with a piston animation and solve-for mode.

-   **[IMF Strength Explorer](./imf-strength-explorer/index.md)**

    ![IMF Strength Explorer](./imf-strength-explorer/imf-strength-explorer.png)

    Compare boiling points across dispersion, dipole, and hydrogen-bonded molecules with an interactive bar chart.

-   **[Integrated Rate Law Grapher](./integrated-rate-law-grapher/index.md)**

    ![Integrated Rate Law Grapher](./integrated-rate-law-grapher/integrated-rate-law-grapher.png)

    Explore zero, first, and second order concentration-time plots simultaneously to identify the linear integrated rate law and extract k.

-   **[Interactive Density Graphing](./density-graphing/index.md)**

    ![Interactive Density Graphing](./density-graphing/density-graphing.png)

    Plot mass vs. volume data, run a best-fit line, and compare the resulting density to known reference materials.

-   **[Interactive Exothermic and Endothermic Energy Diagrams](./energy-diagram-explorer/index.md)**

    ![Interactive Exothermic and Endothermic Energy Diagrams](./energy-diagram-explorer/energy-diagram-explorer.png)

    Adjust activation energy, enthalpy, and catalyst effects to compare exothermic and endothermic energy profiles side-by-side.

-   **[Interactive Heating Curve Simulator](./heating-curve-simulator/index.md)**

    ![Interactive Heating Curve Simulator](./heating-curve-simulator/heating-curve-simulator.png)

    Animate a phase-change heating curve, compare four substances, and visualize how energy is partitioned between temperature changes and latent heat.

-   **[Interactive Lewis Structure Builder](./lewis-structure-builder/index.md)**

    ![Interactive Lewis Structure Builder](./lewis-structure-builder/lewis-structure-builder.png)

    Follow the six-step Lewis structure procedure with live electron tracking and animations.

-   **[Interactive Mass Spectrometer](./mass-spectrometer-sim/index.md)**

    ![Interactive Mass Spectrometer](./mass-spectrometer-sim/mass-spectrometer-sim.png)

    Animated MicroSim showing mass spectrometer stages and matching isotope mass spectra for common AP Chemistry elements.

-   **[Interactive Orbital Shape Visualizer](./orbital-shape-visualizer/index.md)**

    ![Interactive Orbital Shape Visualizer](./orbital-shape-visualizer/orbital-shape-visualizer.png)

    Explore s, p, and d orbital probability maps with adjustable quantum numbers.

-   **[Interactive pH Scale Explorer](./ph-scale-explorer/index.md)**

    ![Interactive pH Scale Explorer](./ph-scale-explorer/ph-scale-explorer.png)

    Drag along a full-color pH scale to compare household substances and convert between pH, pOH, [H⁺], and [OH⁻].

-   **[Interactive Titration Calculator](./titration-calculator/index.md)**

    ![Interactive Titration Calculator](./titration-calculator/titration-calculator.png)

    Visualize strong or weak acid titrations by adjusting concentrations and volumes while watching the equivalence point update live.

-   **[Ionic Crystal Lattice Visualizer](./ionic-lattice-visualizer/index.md)**

    ![Ionic Crystal Lattice Visualizer](./ionic-lattice-visualizer/ionic-lattice-visualizer.png)

    Explore NaCl, MgO, CaF₂, and CsCl lattices with adjustable grid size, rotation, and ion labels.

-   **[Ksp Precipitation Predictor](./ksp-precipitation-predictor/index.md)**

    ![Ksp Precipitation Predictor](./ksp-precipitation-predictor/ksp-precipitation-predictor.png)

    Mix ionic solutions, compare Q vs Ksp for common salts, and visualize precipitation in animated beakers.

-   **[Le Chatelier's Principle Explorer](./le-chatelier-explorer/index.md)**

    ![Le Chatelier's Principle Explorer](./le-chatelier-explorer/le-chatelier-explorer.png)

    Apply concentration, pressure, temperature, and catalyst stresses to the Haber equilibrium while monitoring particles, bar charts, K, Q, and shift direction.

-   **[Limiting Reagent and Percent Yield Calculator](./limiting-reagent-percent-yield/index.md)**

    ![Limiting Reagent and Percent Yield Calculator](./limiting-reagent-percent-yield/limiting-reagent-percent-yield.png)

    Enter reactant masses and actual yield to visualize limiting reagents, theoretical yield, and percent yield with animated bars and gauges.

-   **[Matter Classification Flowchart](./matter-classification-flowchart/index.md)**

    ![Matter Classification Flowchart](./matter-classification-flowchart/matter-classification-flowchart.png)

    Interactive decision tree for classifying matter as elements, compounds, homogeneous mixtures, or heterogeneous mixtures.

-   **[Mechanism Step Explorer](./mechanism-step-explorer/index.md)**

    ![Mechanism Step Explorer](./mechanism-step-explorer/mechanism-step-explorer.png)

    Watch reactants collide, form an intermediate, and regenerate species while tracking concentrations for a two-step catalytic mechanism.

-   **[Microstate Visualizer](./microstate-visualizer/index.md)**

    ![Microstate Visualizer](./microstate-visualizer/microstate-visualizer.png)

    Animate particles in a divided container while tracking microstate counts, probabilities, and entropy.

-   **[MO Energy Diagram Explorer](./mo-energy-diagram/index.md)**

    ![MO Energy Diagram Explorer](./mo-energy-diagram/mo-energy-diagram.png)

    Build MO energy diagrams for diatomic molecules, compute bond order, and analyze magnetism.

-   **[Nernst Equation Explorer](./nernst-equation-explorer/index.md)**

    ![Nernst Equation Explorer](./nernst-equation-explorer/nernst-equation-explorer.png)

    Calculate non-standard cell potentials with interactive concentrations, electron counts, and live E vs log Q plotting.

-   **[PES Spectrum Interactive Visualizer](./pes-spectrum-visualizer/index.md)**

    ![PES Spectrum Interactive Visualizer](./pes-spectrum-visualizer/pes-spectrum-visualizer.png)

    Compare PES spectra for elements H–Ar, toggle annotations, and connect peaks to electron configurations.

-   **[Periodic Trends Explorer](./periodic-trends-explorer/index.md)**

    ![Periodic Trends Explorer](./periodic-trends-explorer/periodic-trends-explorer.png)

    Visualize atomic radius, ionization energy, electron affinity, electronegativity, and Zeff across the periodic table.

-   **[Precision vs Accuracy Target Diagram](./precision-accuracy/index.md)**

    ![Precision vs Accuracy Target Diagram](./precision-accuracy/precision-accuracy.png)

    Interactive visualization demonstrating the difference between precision (repeatability) and accuracy (correctness) in measurements using a target/dart analogy.

-   **[Reaction Type Classifier](./reaction-type-classifier/index.md)**

    ![Reaction Type Classifier](./reaction-type-classifier/reaction-type-classifier.png)

    Practice classifying chemical equations into synthesis, decomposition, single replacement, double replacement, or combustion reactions with instant feedback.

-   **[Scientific Method Workflow](./scientific-method/index.md)**

    ![Scientific Method Workflow](./scientific-method/scientific-method.png)

    Interactive flowchart visualizing each step of the scientific method, from observation and hypothesis formation to experimentation and conclusion.

-   **[Titration Curve Comparison Explorer](./titration-curve-explorer/index.md)**

    ![Titration Curve Comparison Explorer](./titration-curve-explorer/titration-curve-explorer.png)

    Compare strong-strong, weak-strong, and strong-weak acid-base titration curves with annotated features and indicator guidance.

-   **[VSEPR Molecular Geometry Builder](./vsepr-geometry-builder/index.md)**

    ![VSEPR Molecular Geometry Builder](./vsepr-geometry-builder/vsepr-geometry-builder.png)

    Adjust electron groups and lone pairs to visualize VSEPR geometries with animated arrangements.

-   **[Weak Acid ICE Solver](./weak-acid-ice-solver/index.md)**

    ![Weak Acid ICE Solver](./weak-acid-ice-solver/weak-acid-ice-solver.png)

    Enter a weak acid concentration and Ka value to see the full ICE table, Ka algebra, and percent ionization checks side by side.

-   **[Weak Acid ICE Table Solver](./weak-acid-ice-table-solver/index.md)**

    ![Weak Acid ICE Table Solver](./weak-acid-ice-table-solver/weak-acid-ice-table-solver.png)

    Enter a weak acid concentration and Ka to generate a complete ICE table, solve for pH, and visualize percent ionization.

</div>
